SGM College Karad

About US

The college has been started way back in June, 1954 with only 54 students in a single Arts Faculty. It has been flourished over the years to become a multi-faculty institution with 30 departments, a large number of support services and interdisciplinary activities at present. The campus is spread over about 6.8 hectors. The total student strength of Senior and Junior College is more than 12,500. The number of teaching and non-teaching staff is above 400. Now the college is one of the biggest and well known multifacility colleges in the jurisdiction of Shivaji University, Kolhapur.
